Teachers For Childcare

Job description

If you welcome hugs from little children, try your best to keep them safe and comfortable and very much willing to provide them with a nurturing learning environment, we welcome you to come to grow with us at our childcare centre.
A franchisee of the Modern Montessori International (MMI) group, operating at our beautifully revamped kindergarten. Montessori education offers our children opportunities to develop their potential as they step out into the world as engaged, competent, responsible, and respectful citizens with an understanding and appreciation that learning is for life. It also recognizes that children learn in different ways, at different paces, with each advancing through the curriculum as he is ready, guided by the teacher and an individualized learning plan.

Job responsibilities

  • Plan and implement daily programs and activities aligned to the Curriculum Framework
  • Routine care for the children
  • Participation in school activities - year-end concert, excursions, centre's promotional events, parent-teacher meetings and other experiential activities
  • Establish collaborative partnership with families and communities
  • Handling queries and feedbacks from parents
  • Ad-Hoc duties

There is definitely a potential to progress further if you possess the passion, aptitude and commitment for the job.

Qualifications

  • With relevant certificates in Early Childhood Education
  • Cheerful, Creative & Inspiring, Motivating, Patient
  • Keen Learner with a positive attitude
  • A good team player

Other benefits

  • Health, dental, and vision plans, tuition assistance for our employees and their families, paid time off, flexible spending accounts.
  • 2:1 retirement plan contributions, child care centers, and up to $50,000 housing subsidy
  • ICF (International Coach Federation) certified
  • CEAP Certified Employee Assistance Professional desired, but not required.
