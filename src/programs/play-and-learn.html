Play and Learn

In HappyKids, children learn best through play. They are motivated by the joy of learning new things and exploring their world. When they are happy and having fun, they are more likely to be engaged in their learning and remember what they have learned.HappyKids is a time for children to develop a love of learning. It is a time when they are discovering who they are and what they can do. They learn best when they are allowed to explore and play. When they are given the freedom to be creative and try new things, they are more likely to be motivated and engaged in their learning.

Class Description

The joy of learning doesn’t just happen in the classroom – it happens at home too. Parents can encourage their child’s love of learning by providing opportunities for them to explore and discover new things. There are many resources available online and in libraries that can help parents support their child’s learning at home. By creating a positive environment where learning is valued, parents can help their child develop a lifelong love of learning.
A Play and Learn class is a type of HappyKids class that emphasizes playtime as a way to learn. This means that instead of traditional sit-down learning, your child will be encouraged to explore, experiment, and use their imaginations through play.

Why choose a Play and Learn class?

There are many benefits to choosing a Play and Learn class for your child’s education. Here are just a few:

  • Play is an important part of early childhood development. It helps children learn essential life skills like problem-solving, cooperation, and communication.
  • Playtime gives children the opportunity to expend energy and be active, which can help them focus when it’s time for more structured activities.
  • A Play and Learn environment fosters creativity and imagination. This can lead to increased confidence and self-esteem as children discover they can succeed at new things.
